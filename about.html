— About the Studio

A Hickory custom builder,
established in 2005.

Higgins Building Group, Inc. is a custom home builder based in Hickory, North Carolina, specializing in luxury residential construction across Western North Carolina. With over twenty years of experience, we deliver fully custom homes tailored to each client's vision.

— 01 / Statement

Premium residential construction with attention to detail and refined execution.

Higgins Building Group is known for its client-focused building process, high-quality craftsmanship, and energy-efficient construction practices. Our work is centered on a single discipline — designing and building exceptional luxury homes from the ground up.

We have an established presence across Western North Carolina, with projects in Hickory, Lake James, and surrounding communities including Newton, Conover, Maiden, Morganton, and Lincolnton. Whether the build is in-town or set in the mountain and lake communities of the foothills, our process and standard remain the same.

Clients are involved at every stage, from early design decisions through final walkthroughs, with continuous communication throughout construction.
