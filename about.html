— About the Studio

A residential building
practice rooted in care.

Higgins Building Group is a custom home builder based in Hickory, North Carolina. We bring decades of construction experience and a deeply personal approach to every project — from first design conversation to final walkthrough.

— 01 / Statement

"Honest communication. Painstaking attention. On time, on budget."

Our work begins and ends with the client. We listen first — to how a family lives, what a place is meant to feel like at six in the morning and ten at night, where the light should fall, what budget the project actually has to honor.

From there we build. Methodically, transparently, and with the same level of finish whether the home is a 3,000-square-foot in-town residence or a custom mountain retreat. We hold ourselves accountable to the details that other builders quietly let slip — because those are the details our clients live with for the next thirty years.

The result is a home that arrives on schedule, on budget, and at a quality of craftsmanship that earns repeat referrals from the only people who really know: the families who've already moved in.

— 02 / Principles How We Work

Six commitments that shape every build.

01

Collaborative Design

We work alongside your architect — or help you find one — and stay involved from concept through final detail. Construction begins on a plan everyone agrees on.

02

Transparent Pricing

Open-book budgets, line-item allowances, no surprises. You know what each decision costs before it's made, and you know why.

03

Disciplined Scheduling

Tight subcontractor sequencing keeps crews moving without idle days. Most clients move in on the date we set at groundbreaking.

04

Quality Control

Daily on-site presence by ownership. Every trade is inspected; every punch item is closed before the next phase begins.

05

Energy Performance

ENERGY STAR-rated construction is our default, not an upgrade. Better envelope, better systems, lower utility bills — built in.

06

Long Relationships

Our standard one-year inspection is just the beginning. We answer the phone five years later — and ten — for the homes we've built.
