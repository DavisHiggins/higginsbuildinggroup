— 01 / The Studio

Building the homes our clients imagine — and the ones they couldn't have imagined alone.

Higgins Building Group is a Hickory-based custom residential builder devoted to a single discipline: designing and constructing exceptional luxury homes from the ground up.

Every project begins with deliberate process — early collaboration with the client and architect, transparent budgeting, and uncompromising quality control. It ends with a residence that reflects the people who live in it. We measure our work by craftsmanship, communication, and the relationships we maintain long after the keys are handed over.

Our footprint covers Hickory and the surrounding foothills, the high country of Western North Carolina, and the lake communities in between.
