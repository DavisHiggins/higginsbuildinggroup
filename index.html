— 01 / The Studio

A Hickory-based custom home builder, focused on a single discipline.

Higgins Building Group, Inc. designs and builds fully custom luxury homes across Western North Carolina — from initial concept through completion.

Our practice is centered on premium residential construction with attention to detail, quality materials, and refined architectural execution. Every project is client-driven: clients are involved at every stage, from early design decisions through final walkthroughs, with continuous communication throughout construction.

Established in 2005, we serve Hickory, Catawba County, Lake James, and the surrounding foothills, mountain, and lake communities of Western North Carolina.
